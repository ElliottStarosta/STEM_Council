What We Do

The STEM Council was established by the Head of STEM to create a thriving ecosystem of events, activities, and resources for Lions at Earl of March. We organize and support the STEM community, connecting students with opportunities to explore their passions and discover new interests.

Our Impact

At Earl of March S.S. in Ontario, we're proud to support a diverse range of STEM programs including Computer Science, Computer Engineering, and more. Our council ensures that every Lion has the opportunity to explore what STEM truly means.
